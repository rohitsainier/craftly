Introduction

Editra ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our macOS application.

By using Editra, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email address - If you create an account or contact support
  • Support communications - Messages and feedback you send us
  • Payment information - Processed securely through Apple's payment system

Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous usage statistics - Only if you opt-in to analytics
  • Crash reports - To improve app stability (optional)
  • Device information - macOS version and app version for compatibility

Important: We never collect or store your media files, videos, images, or audio content. All processing happens locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our service - Ensure the app functions properly
  • Improve user experience - Understand how features are used
  • Send important updates - Notify you of app updates or security issues
  • Provide customer support - Respond to your questions and requests
  • Detect and prevent issues - Identify and fix bugs and technical problems
  • Comply with legal obligations - Meet regulatory requirements

Local Data Processing

Editra is designed with privacy at its core. Here's what that means:

All Processing is Local

Video, image, and audio processing happens entirely on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud.

No File Uploads

We do NOT upload your media files to our servers under any circumstances.

Your Content Stays Yours

All generated content remains on your Mac. You have full control.

Works Offline

After installation, Editra works completely offline. No internet required for core features.

Third-Party Services

We work with limited third-party services to provide our service:

Payment Processing

Apple - All payments are processed through the App Store. We never see your payment information.

Analytics

None by default - We respect your privacy. Analytics are optional and fully anonymous if you opt-in.

Crash Reporting

Optional and anonymous - Helps us fix bugs. You can disable this in settings.

Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Local storage - All your generated content is stored on your Mac
  • No selling of data - We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information
  • Regular audits - We conduct security audits to ensure your data is protected

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • Sending you an email notification (if you've provided your email)
  • Showing an in-app notification about the changes

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
